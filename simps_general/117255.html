If one was to make the assertion that every episode in the classic era was a flawless masterpiece that were individually better than anything else ever produced, I would agree that that would be overrating them. That is going several steps too far.

However, in the context of the era having consistently high quality, holding up as entertaining television that is rightfully praised for its humour and storytelling, and generally better than pretty much anything that comes in what’s come to be defined as the post-classic era, I would say that’s a fair assertion and not overrating it at all.
